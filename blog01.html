Creating an excellent online presence for Web Developers

Julia Gongala

Sep 25, 2022 7 min

Would you like to work for the world’s leading companies while making an impact through beautiful front-end development? You’ve got a skills, but you are struggling to get noticed? This article is for you because there is a high demand for exceptional artists on the internet today and this guide will show you how to get started and how to create an excellent online presence for front-end developers to get you notice by potential developers.


What do we mean by online presence?

Since I am going to focus on importance of online presence, you might ask what exactly it is, and what do we understand by online presence. Nowadays more and more people when they look for product or they look for services, the common place that they look for it is on the Internet. So, it is very important that each and every single company has some form of presence withing the Internet. Marketing company, TrustWokz, defines online presence as a living and breathing essence of your business on the web. The second thing to consider when talking about online presence is that weather you have a website or your social media, there is some form of information that is being written out there about you, your company, or about anything that you do so you always need to choose whether you want to be reactive or do you want to be proactive in the sense, do you want to tell your own story, introduce yourself, your brand or product. This is the basic reason why you should get involved and work on your online presence. Build and manage online presence so the world will find a vibrant and healthy presence when they bump into you online.

  1. Linkedin

    Platform that was launched in 2003 and currently they are millions of users worldwide. Therefore, you need to work on your profile to stand out and the reason to do that is to get better and higher job opportunities.

    Recruiters are using LinkedIn to look for candidates and they are looking at thousands of candidates every single day therefore they spent really little time on everybody’s profile and the very first thing that they are looking at is a picture. You need to have professional picture. The next important factor on LinkedIn is headline. A lot of people are looking at headlines. Headline is usually defaulted to your current job but there are 120 characters that you should definitely take advantage of so first the first thing that include in your headline should be your job description, the company you are working for and also something that describes you, your speciality.

    Another part of creating good profile, is summary. People usually focus on first four lines, so make sure you have the most important information in there. Also make sure that you will include your contact information. In summary you have the opportunity to upload something or provide a link, so as a web developer, make sure you add a link to your portfolio website or GitHub, so people will be able to see your work examples or your projects.


  2. Twitter

    You might think why I would suggest you use Twitter. So, Twitter has been around for a long time and is very active platform. It offers you big engagement from your audience that you could share information about your new projects, or what is happening currently in your life, what you are working on.


  3. GitHub

    GitHub is really crucial part of getting hired as a self-taught programmer. Your repository on GitHub will allow the interviewer to do a lot more in-depth research on you and your work. They could check your code, projects, but also how much have you been doing, how active and commit you are in your work. Your repository will represent you, and it will show what you have been practicing and how ambitious you are. On GitHub you will show your interest in different programming languages, and it could be an extra point for you, if you are practicing new frameworks after hours.


  4. StackOverflow

    StackOverflow is a good platform to learn and help others. It is always good to show your future employer that you are not scared to ask for help or you are happy to help others.


If you still do not own accounts on these websites, create profile and make it look professional and skilful. Using social media will let you to present your skills and be active on the Internet.
